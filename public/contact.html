Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get help?

We aim to connect seniors with volunteers within 2-3 business days of receiving a request.

Is there a cost for services?

No, all ENW services are provided free of charge by volunteer community members.

What if I need immediate help?

For emergency situations, please call 911. For urgent but non-emergency needs, call our main line and press 1.

Service Area

ENW currently serves the greater Vilnius area, including:

  • Old Town (Senamiestis)
  • Antakalnis
  • Žirmūnai
  • Šnipiškės
  • Užupis
  • And surrounding neighborhoods

Expanding to new areas regularly based on volunteer availability.
