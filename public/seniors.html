How We Can Help

Our network of screened volunteers can assist with:

  • Grocery shopping and delivery
  • Prescription pickup
  • Transportation to medical appointments
  • Light household tasks
  • Companionship and social visits
  • Help with technology
  • Pet care assistance
  • Other tasks to help you stay independent

All services are provided free of charge by caring community volunteers.

Request Submitted Successfully!

Thank you for reaching out. We'll review your request and connect you with a suitable volunteer in your area within 2-3 business days. You'll receive a call or email to confirm the match and next steps.

If you need immediate assistance or have urgent needs, please call our helpline at (555) 123-4567.

What Happens Next?

  1. Review: We'll review your request within 24 hours
  2. Match: We'll find a suitable volunteer in your area
  3. Contact: You'll receive a call/email about your match
  4. Meet: An initial meeting to ensure compatibility
  5. Ongoing Support: Regular check-ins to ensure everything goes well

Your Safety is Our Priority

  • All volunteers undergo background screening
  • References are checked and verified
  • Regular check-ins ensure positive experiences
  • You can request a different volunteer at any time
  • Emergency contacts are always available
