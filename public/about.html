About Elderly Neighbour Watch

Building bridges between generations through compassionate community care

Our Story

Elderly Neighbour Watch was founded in 2023 with a simple but powerful vision: no elderly person should face daily challenges alone when caring neighbors are just around the corner.

Our founder, inspired by her own grandmother's struggles with isolation and daily tasks, recognized that many communities have both elderly residents who need support and caring individuals who want to helpâ€”but no easy way to connect them.

What started as informal neighborhood helping has grown into a structured network that ensures reliable, ongoing support while building meaningful relationships between generations.

How ENW Works

Safe Requests

Seniors, family members, or care providers can request help through our secure platform. We collect essential information while respecting privacy and ensuring consent.

Careful Matching

Our coordinators match requests with volunteers based on location, availability, skills, and personal compatibility to ensure the best possible partnerships.

Ongoing Support

We provide continuous coordination, check-ins, and resources to ensure both seniors and volunteers have positive, safe experiences.

Community Building

Beyond individual assistance, we organize group activities and events that strengthen neighborhood connections and combat social isolation.

Our Values

  • Dignity: Every person deserves respect and autonomy
  • Safety: We prioritize secure, verified interactions
  • Community: Stronger neighborhoods benefit everyone
  • Compassion: Kindness is at the heart of all we do

Services We Coordinate

  • Grocery shopping and delivery
  • Medication pickup
  • Transportation to appointments
  • Light household tasks
  • Companionship visits
  • Technology assistance
  • Pet care support

Our Impact

Reducing Isolation

Regular volunteer visits and assistance help combat loneliness and keep seniors connected to their communities.

Supporting Independence

By helping with essential tasks, we enable seniors to remain in their homes and maintain their autonomy longer.

Building Relationships

Many of our matches develop into lasting friendships that enrich the lives of both seniors and volunteers.

Strengthening Communities

Our network creates more connected, caring neighborhoods where people look out for one another.
