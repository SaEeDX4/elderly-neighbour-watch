Volunteer Process

  1. Apply: Submit this registration form
  2. Interview: Brief phone/video call
  3. Background Check: We verify your information
  4. Training: Learn best practices and safety
  5. Matching: Connect with your first senior
  6. Support: Ongoing guidance and check-ins

Time Commitment

  • Minimum: 2 hours per week
  • Typical: 3-5 hours per week
  • Flexibility: Schedule around your availability
  • Duration: 6-month minimum commitment preferred

We understand life changes, and we're flexible with scheduling adjustments.

Volunteer Support

  • Comprehensive orientation training
  • 24/7 coordinator support
  • Monthly volunteer meetups
  • Mileage reimbursement available
  • Volunteer appreciation events
  • Professional references provided

Safety Guidelines

  • Never provide personal financial information
  • All first meetings should be coordinated through ENW
  • Report any concerns to coordinators immediately
  • Follow established safety protocols
  • Trust your instincts and communicate openly
