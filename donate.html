Donation FAQ

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, ENW is a registered non-profit organization. You'll receive a tax-deductible receipt for all donations.

How is my donation used?

85% goes directly to program costs (volunteer screening, training, coordination). 15% covers administrative expenses.

Can I donate in honor of someone?

Yes! Contact us at donate@elderlynw.org to arrange memorial or honor donations.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never store payment details on our servers.
